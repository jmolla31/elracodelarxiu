Welcome to El Racó de l'Arxiu! This page provides information about the project, its mission, and the team behind it.

## Our Mission

El Racó de l'Arxiu is dedicated to preserving and sharing the musical heritage of our community. We work to digitize historical documents, recordings, and photographs to ensure they remain accessible for future generations.
